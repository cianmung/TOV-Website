OneAdvisor Home

An API based collaboration among multiple partners in the property ecosystem, OneAdvisor Home is a one-stop portal that helps property buyers make informed purchase decisions. Besides detailing each step of the home buying process, buyers are provided with a seamless financing journey as they learn what are the types of property they can afford, bank loan options and how to use their Central Provident Fund balances. Eventually, they can connect with a mortgage specialist for a more personalised consultation.

Going above most general advisory tools in the market, OneAdvisor Home’s affordability tool can delve deep to provide more specific details such as down payment, stamp duty as well as legal fees payable.
